Catering Info:

We Take Any Size Groups

Here at Cozy Corner, we're prepared, and happy, to serve any size needed for catering. All we ask is that you give us an estimate of the size so that we can adequately serve your needs!

24hr Notice

In order for us to prepare your food to perfection, we request that you give us, at minimum, a 24-hour notice on any catering orders you would like to place. We thank you for your understanding.

Pricing

Pricing varies on certain food items, sizing, and timing. We work around every budget, however, and would love to give you an evaluation! See the link below to talk to a Cozy Corner employee about pricing.

Discounts and Bundles

Depending on what you order, you could be eligible for getting your order at a discounted price! Cozy Corner offers bundles and coupons as well, see the link below to receive more information!

Dietary Options and Customizations

Cozy Corner is proud to offer Vegetarian, Vegan, and Gluten-Free food options. If there is a certain food allergy that needs to be taken into consideration, please contact us so that we may work with your needs while still providing delectable dishes that everyone can enjoy.
